EAAIS was created to bridge the gap between advanced AI tools and the everyday needs of East Anglia businesses. We started with one goal: make AI accessible, practical and profitable for local business.

What we're building toward

We're building a trusted AI partner for the region — assisting businesses automate work, improve efficiency, and unlock growth with solutions that actually fit their day-to-day operations.

East Anglia is home to ambitious businesses, but many still lack accessible AI support. Our local focus lets us deliver relevant, on-site and industry-aware solutions that larger firms often miss.

Who's behind it

A small team of AI engineers based in East Anglia who'd rather visit your office than send a deck. We work hands-on with regional SMEs across Norfolk, Suffolk, Cambridgeshire and Essex, and stay accountable for what we ship.

  • AI agents that drive software end-to-end the way a person would.

Workflow Automation

Predictive Intelligence

Machine-learning models that forecast demand, flag anomalies, and surface opportunities — turning your historical data into decisions for next week, not next quarter.

  • AI automation that learns from your past to predict your near future.

Production deployments of large language models grounded in your own documents, with citations, guardrails, and a clean way to roll back when an answer is wrong.

Data Pipeline Architecture

Real-time data pipelines that collect, clean, and route information across every system you run — so dashboards, AI models, and reports all draw from one trusted source.

  • The plumbing that makes AI automation reliable: clean data, in the right place, on time.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · This policy explains how cookies and similar storage are used on eastangliaaiservices.co.uk.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. Similar technologies (localStorage, sessionStorage) work the same way. They allow sites to remember settings and improve your experience.

2. Cookies We Use

This site is intentionally lightweight and uses only strictly necessary storage. We do not use third-party trackers, advertising cookies, or cross-site analytics by default.

  • eaais_cookie_consent — records your choice from the cookie banner so we don't show it again. Strictly necessary. Stored for 12 months.
  • eaais_chat_history — optionally stores your ARIA chat session locally so messages persist if you reload. Strictly necessary if you use the chat. Stored for 30 days or until you clear browser data.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not currently load third-party advertising or analytics cookies. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and consent will be requested via the cookie banner before any non-essential cookies are set.

4. Managing Cookies

You can change your consent at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer. You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings — guidance is available at ico.org.uk/your-data-matters/online.

ARIA runs entirely in your browser and uses only local storage. No chat content is sent to our servers or any third party.
